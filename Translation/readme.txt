Here everybody can help to localize this plugin to other languages.

The corresponding languages are:
values-ca: Catalan
values-cs: Czech
values-de: German
values-es: Spanish
values-fr: French
values-hu: Hungary
values-in: Indonesian
values-it: Italian
values-ja: Japanese
values-ko: Korean
values-ms: Malay, Indonesian
values-nl: Dutch
values-pl: Polish
values-pt: Portuguese
values-pr: Pirate
values-ru: Russian
values-sr: Serbian
values-th: Thai
values-tr: Turkish
values-uk: Ukrainian
values-zh-rTW: Traditional Chinese
values-zh: Chinese
values: English (default language)

How translation works:
You have just to translate the part after colon ("tags": "Your Translation"). Special characters like \n might be used to indicate a new line. When your translation is done, make sure to create a 'pull request' so it can be merged to main branch. If you forget to do that step your translation won't be added to the main branch.
